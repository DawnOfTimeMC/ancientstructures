## 🏛️ Ancient Structures Forge/Fabric 1.20.1 [v0.0.14]

![ancient structures achievements](https://i.imgur.com/442h5XV.png)

- Fixed lone_priest and windmill structures not spawning (corrupted NBT re-exported)
